Final Year Project
Jul 2020 – Oct 2020
Politeknik Ibrahim Sultan

Developed a smart home automation system that allows remote and voice-controlled operation of electrical appliances such as lights, fans, and sockets using IoT technology. The system uses NodeMCU ESP8266 and Arduino Uno microcontrollers, integrated with Blynk and Google Assistant via IFTTT, to control AC and DC-powered devices.
